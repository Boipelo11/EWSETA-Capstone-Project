Welcome To The EWSETA
Career Guidance Portal

At our core,we are an organization which empowers young people to make great career choices. The EWSETA aims to guide those who want to pursue careers in the Energy, Water, Future, Artisan related sectors!

About Us

Governance

As one of South Africa's 21 SETAs, the Energy and Water Sector Education and Training Authority (EWSETA) was established in terms of Section 9 of the Skills Development Act (SDA), 97 of 1998, as amended and reports to the Honourable Minister of Higher Education, Science and Technology.


Skills

We equip young people with valuable knowledge which helps them to harness their skills through extensive outreach programmes such as learnerships, internships and Indaba events.


Partnerships

We partner with commendable organizations which may be your future employers, hosts on practicals and funders. Opportunities are limitless and we are here to guide you through them.

Career Finder

Browse through to find a career you may like

Energy

The energy industry is involved in the production, distribution, and consumption of various forms of energy, including fossil fuels, renewable energy, and nuclear energy. This industry is focused on providing energy to power homes, businesses, and transportation. Careers in this industry include engineers, technicians, and managers in areas such as oil and gas, renewable energy, and electric power generation and distribution..

Learn More

Water

The water industry involves the management, treatment, and distribution of water for public and private use. This includes the provision of safe drinking water, wastewater treatment, and management of water resources. Careers in this industry include water engineers, wastewater operators, hydrologists, and water resource managers.

Learn More

Future

The "future" industry is not a specific industry, but rather a collection of emerging fields that are focused on creating new technologies, products, and services that will shape the future. These fields include artificial intelligence, robotics, biotechnology, nanotechnology, and others. Careers in these fields are focused on research, development, and innovation.

Learn More

Artisan

The artisan industry is focused on creating handmade goods using traditional techniques and materials. This industry includes a wide range of crafts, such as woodworking, pottery, jewelry-making, and textile production. Careers in this industry are typically self-employed or work for small businesses, and include artisans, designers, and craftspeople.
